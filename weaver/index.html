Welcome

Weaver Demolition has been operating in the demolition and dismantling industry for 40 years. Our client base includes established contractors within the construction and petrochemical industry. We also provide demolition services for local councils, architects, consultants and private clients where we can act as principle contractors. Based near Bristol, we can undertake projects of various sizes throughout the UK and can provide advice and solutions for a wide range of demolition issues. We are committed to upholding our reputation for high standards of safety, professionalism and efficiency. Environmentally aware, we recycle materials from our projects where possible and are able to provide sales of railway sleepers, salvaged building materials and crushed concrete.
